About us

Russian Threat Tracker provides a near-real time view of Russian military threats in Ukraine drawing from on-the-ground sources, social media, and data analytics of available data sources. It is built and continuously improved by Janes and a team of professionals from software companies, product design firms, and leading geospatial, mapping, and signals technology companies, as well as former members of the military and intelligence community.

This work is not being done in cooperation of any government. Russian Threat Tracker is intended to help the Ukrainian people protect themselves. We Stand with Ukraine.

Disclaimer

The information on this application is unofficial and obtained from crowdsourcing and open source information. It is provided to promote awareness of the location of Russian forces in Ukraine, but due to the dynamic nature of the war, the accuracy of the data cannot be guaranteed. Please use at your own risk.
